## 1. VERIFIED FACTS

### City Overview
- **Population**: ~108,000 (2024 est.) — Cabarrus County seat
- **Location**: ~25 miles NE of Uptown Charlotte via I-85; outer-ring Charlotte suburb
- **Character**: Historic downtown + rapidly developing suburban/exurban growth; NASCAR hub (Charlotte Motor Speedway)
- **Growth**: Fast (25%+ since 2010)

### Hospitals with Maternity Services

| Hospital | Location | NICU Level | Verified Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Atrium Health Cabarrus** | 920 Church St N, Concord (central) | **Level III NICU** | Wikipedia; Atrium Health official site confirms 457-bed acute-care hospital with Jeff Gordon Children's Hospital on campus |
| **Atrium Health Carolinas Medical Center** | Uptown Charlotte (~25 mi SW via I-85) | **Level IV NICU** (regional perinatal center) | Atrium Health system data |
| **Novant Health Matthews** | Matthews (~18 mi SW) | **Level II NICU** | Novant Health official data |

**NICU note**: Atrium Health Cabarrus has a verified Level III NICU — can care for babies born before 32 weeks and those with significant medical needs. For the most complex cases (Level IV), patients transfer to Atrium HealthCarolinas Medical Center in Uptown Charlotte (~25-30 min drive). Jeff Gordon Children's Hospital (opened 2006) is co-located on the Cabarrus campus.

### Birth Centers
- **No freestanding birth centers in Concord/Cabarrus County.**
- Nearest AABC-listed birth centers are in Charlotte or Chapel Hill:
  - Aya Birth & Community Wellness — Charlotte
  - Haven Women's Health and Birth Center — Charlotte area
  - Natural Beginnings Birth & Wellness Center — NC
  - North Carolina Birth Center — Chapel Hill (~130 mi NE)
- **This birth center gap is a major differentiator for the page.**

### Doula Landscape
- Small local doula community — fewer than 5 dedicated doula practices with "Concord" in branding
- Charlotte-based collectives serve Concord: Charlotte Doulas, Queen City Doulas
- DONA International, ProDoula, CAPPA active from Charlotte
- "doula Concord NC" is a thin SERP — most content targets "Charlotte doula"

### Medicaid / Doula Coverage (NC)
- **NC Medicaid covers doula services** — same framework as Cary page
- Coverage includes: prenatal visits, labor & delivery support, postpartum visits
- Reimbursement: Fee-for-service through NC Medicaid Direct and Standard Plans
- Doulas must complete state-approved training and enroll through NC Medicaid (NCTracks)
- MCOs in Cabarrus area: Carolina Complete Health, UnitedHealthcare Community Plan, WellCare

### Local Anchors
- **Frank Liske Park** — 76 acres; disc golf, tennis, volleyball, walking trails — South Concord
- **Vietnam Veterans Park** — walking trail, playground, dog park
- **Downtown Concord** — revitalized Main Street district
- **Charlotte Motor Speedway** — NASCAR hub; regional identity anchor
- **Cabarrus Parks & Recreation** — Camp Tuscarora, Irish Creek Greenway
- **Traffic**: I-85 primary corridor to Charlotte. Rush-hour drive: 35-50 min; off-peak: ~25 min. US-74 alternate route.

### Cost Range
- **Doula services (Concord/Charlotte area)**: $800–$2,500 for birth doula package
- **Postpartum doula**: $25–$45/hour
- **Hospital birth (vaginal, uninsured)**: $10,000–$18,000+ at Atrium Health Cabarrus
- **NC Medicaid**: Covers 100% for eligible patients; doula services now included
