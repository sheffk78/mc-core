## 1. VERIFIED FACTS

### City Overview
- **Population**: ~75,000 (city); ~525,000 (Greenville County metro) — one of the SE's fastest-growing metros
- **Location**: Upstate South Carolina, foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains; I-85 corridor between Charlotte and Atlanta
- **Character**: Thriving downtown (Falls Park on the Reedy), strong wellness/holistic scene, major healthcare hub (Prisma Health, Bon Secours)
- **Growth**: Fast (18%+ since 2010)

### Doula Landscape
- Active local doula community with several practices serving the Upstate SC area
- Holistic/wellness-friendly city — strong natural birth community
- DONA International, CAPPA, and local trainings active
- Greenville Doulas and similar collectives serve the area
- Not saturated — room for a well-structured resource page

### Medicaid / Doula Coverage (South Carolina)
- **South Carolina Medicaid has expanded doula coverage** — SC implemented Medicaid doula reimbursement (as of 2024 legislation)
- SC Department of Health and Human Services (SCDHHS) covers doula services for eligible pregnancies
- Specific reimbursement rates and enrolled provider details should be verified on SCDHHS website
- This is a **differentiator** from neighboring Georgia (which does NOT cover doulas)

### Local Anchors
- **Falls Park on the Reedy** — iconic downtown waterfall park, signature Greenville identity
- **Swamp Rabbit Trail** — 22-mile multi-use greenway from Greenville to Travelers Rest (stroller-friendly!)
- **Cleveland Park** — family park with zoo, playgrounds
- **Heritage Green** — cultural district: Greenville County Museum of Art, Upcountry History Museum
- **Traffic**: I-85 and I-385 primary corridors; Woodruff Road bottleneck during rush; downtown hospital area well-connected via I-385
- **Airport**: Greenville-Spartanburg International (GSP) — ~15 min east

### Cost Range
- **Doula services**: $800-$2,000 for birth doula package (lower end than Atlanta/Charlotte metros)
- **Postpartum doula**: $25-$40/hour
- **Hospital birth**: $8,000-$15,000+ vaginal delivery without complications
